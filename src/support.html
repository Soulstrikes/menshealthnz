Support Groups

Prostate Cancer Foundation Support Groups offer free, confidential, and understanding spaces for men diagnosed with prostate cancer, along with their partners, families, and whƒÅnau. Across New Zealand, trained volunteer coordinators provide both local and online support to help people navigate the challenges of diagnosis, treatment, and recovery.

Some groups meet regularly, while others connect through Prost-FIT classes or social gatherings. Every group is unique, but all share a common goal ‚Äî to provide encouragement, information, and a sense of community through shared experience.
